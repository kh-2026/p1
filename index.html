A Family Story

My name is Khaled. Before the war, I lived a normal life. I worked, provided for my family, and believed that stability was something permanent.

Everything changed suddenly. What took years to build disappeared in moments.

About My Family

I am a father of five children. Their ages range from early childhood to early teenage years.

For their safety, I had to make the hardest decision of my life — sending my family away while I stayed behind.

Distance is not just physical. It is emotional, psychological, and deeply painful.
